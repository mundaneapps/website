Support

Have a question, ran into a bug, or want to request a feature? Email us at info@mundaneapps.com — a real person reads every message, usually within a couple of days.

Feature requests

Our apps are small and deliberately quiet — no ads, no growth hacking. If there's a cadence type, notification behavior, or workflow you wish Heads-Up supported, tell us. The in-app feature-request form (Settings → Feedback) sends straight to the same inbox.

Account and data questions

For anything about your account, your data, or deleting it, see our Privacy Policy and the delete-account page, or just email us directly.

Subscriptions & billing

Subscriptions are billed and managed through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store — including cancellations and refunds. You can manage or cancel anytime from your store account:

  • Manage subscriptions on Google Play
  • Manage subscriptions on the App Store
