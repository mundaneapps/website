Frequently asked

The short, honest answers.

Do your apps track me or show ads?

No ads, and nothing is ever used to track you across apps or build an advertising profile. To improve the app we offer optional, privacy-friendly analytics (PostHog) and crash reporting (Sentry) — both EU-hosted, anonymous, off by default, and switchable anytime in Settings → Privacy. This website itself has no trackers or analytics at all.

Do I need an account?

No. Our apps work fully offline, forever, with no account. Signing in is optional and only unlocks features that genuinely need the cloud, like backup and cross-device sync.

What does "cost-effective" mean here?

A genuinely useful free tier, with optional paid plans billed through the App Store or Google Play. No dark patterns, no surprise charges — and deleting your account or cancelling is always straightforward.

When will Heads-Up be available?

Heads-Up is in active development and launching soon on Google Play, with the App Store to follow. Watch this space, or email us and we'll let you know.

Where can I read your privacy policy?

Right here: Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We wrote them to be specific and truthful about exactly what our apps do.
