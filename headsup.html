Nothing important catches you off guard.

Heads-Up keeps track of the things in life that need a little lead time, from bills and birthdays to renewals and car maintenance, and gives you a nudge early enough to actually do something about them.

Coming soon toGoogle Play Coming soon toApp Store
Why it helps

The important stuff is scattered everywhere.

Birthdays sit in your contacts. Bills live in your banking app. The car service is in your head, and the renewal notice is buried in your inbox. Nothing warns you early enough to prepare, so things slip. Sometimes that is a forgotten birthday. Sometimes it is a late fee or a lapsed policy.

A nudge, in good time

Heads-Up tells you what is coming with enough lead time to act, not a reminder the day it is already due.

Everything in one place

Bills, birthdays, renewals, and maintenance in a single calm list, instead of scattered across a dozen apps.

One less thing to hold

It keeps track so you do not have to. You stop half-remembering, and stop worrying you have forgotten something.

What if everything that mattered lived in one place, and gave you a nudge early enough to actually do something?

That is the whole idea. The promise is peace of mind, not another list to feel guilty about.

How it is different

Not another to-do list.

To-do apps show what is on your plate. Calendars show what is on your schedule. Heads-Up shows what is coming, with enough time to prepare for it.

How it works

Three simple ways to keep track.

Most things you need to remember fall into one of three rhythms. Heads-Up handles each one the way it actually behaves in real life.

1

One-time

A single thing to handle, like sending flowers or filing a document. When it needs a next step, Heads-Up can carry the thread forward instead of letting it drop.

2

On a schedule

Things that come around on a fixed date, like a monthly bill or a yearly renewal. Surfaced every cycle, in good time, so a miss never costs you.

3

After you last did it

Things you repeat when needed, like changing the oil every six months. The next nudge is counted from the day you actually did it, not a date on paper.

Built with care

The small things that make it work.

Notes that remember why

Every item keeps a running thread of notes, so you always have the context and never lose the reason it mattered.

Follow-ups that carry on

When something is not truly finished, Heads-Up keeps the thread going so the next step does not quietly slip away.

Reminders that arrive on time

Local notifications fire exactly when they should, with a home-screen widget for a glance at what is next.

Yours, and offline

Works fully offline with no account. Optional backup and sync are there when you want them, and never before.
