Our vision

Software should be a good houseguest.

The name is the promise. We make apps for the mundane, the recurring, the easy-to-forget. They stay out of the way, keep your data yours, and do one job so well you stop thinking about it.

Private by design

No ads and no third-party tracking, ever. Optional analytics and crash reporting are anonymous, EU-hosted, and off by default. You choose, and you can leave with your data anytime.

Works offline

Our apps run fully offline, forever, with no account required. Sign-in and sync are optional, added only when they genuinely help you.

Fair and honest

A genuinely useful free tier, with optional subscriptions to keep the lights on. Never dark patterns to trap you.

Clean and polished

Calm, uncluttered interfaces that feel at home on iOS and Android, in light and dark mode, and get out of your way.

Few things, done well

We would rather build a handful of tools with care than a pile of them loudly. Depth over noise, always.
